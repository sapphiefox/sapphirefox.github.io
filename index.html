What is the FSS?

The Freelancing Student Society (FSS) is a student-run community at Noosham University meant for anyone with an interest in the opportunites of freelancing.

We run several academic and social events each semester, providing valuable networking opportunities for everyone!

Our Goals

As a registered club at Noosham University, we strive to provide the best possible experience for all our members, particularly in regards to:

Committment

We will deliver the highest quality experience for all our members, and will provide weekly updates in the world of freelancing

Opportunity

Provide the opportunity for all members to participate in and engage with events and activities, as well as equal opportunities to discuss with key businesses and members of the professional freelancing world

Education

Whether completely new or already engaging with the fascinating world of freelancing, we will teach useful skills, basic and advanced, to improve your skills
